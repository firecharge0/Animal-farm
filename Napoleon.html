Napoleon

Napoleon is an allegory for Joseph Stalin. He is portrayed as a selfish and narcissitic communist pig. He uses a group of dogs to control the farm through fear. He gets rid of Snowball because he was a threat to Napoleons position and power. His main roll in the story is to be the antagonist and dictator. Almost all of his action affect the rest of the animals adversly while helping only the other pigs and dogs.

"But suddenly the dogs sitting around Napoleon let out a deep, menacing growls, and the pigs fell silent and sat down again."
