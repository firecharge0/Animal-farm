Benjamin

Intelectuals during and after the revolution that could do nothing about stalins reign. He is represented as a cynical and inteligent animal who does not comment on the decisions made by Napoleon and his dictatorship. His main role in the novel is to ridicule the decisions of the ignorant animals.

"Old Benjamin, the donkey, seemed quite unchanged since the Rebellion. He did his work in the same slow obstinate way as he had done it in Jones's time, never shirking and never volunteering for extra work either. About the Rebellion and its results he would express no opinion."
