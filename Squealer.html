Squealer

Squealer is an allegory for the propaganda during the russian revolution. He constantly shows the other animals statistics and graphs which falsely state that the production of food is increasing. His main roll in the story is to highlight Napoleons lies and make the animals fall in line by making them beleive that things are better than they are. For example, he changes what is written in the seven commandments of Animalism to suit the pigs wants.

"Squealer, temporarily stunned, was sprawling besides it (the seven commandments), and near at hand there lay a lantern, a paint-brush, and an overturned pot of white paint.
