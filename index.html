Animal Farm

Animal Farm is a book Written by George Orwell in 1945, published in egland.

The book is an allegory for the russian revolution. It tells the story of a group of farm animals under the rule of humans. Mr. Jones the human treats his animals sloppily and treats them like slaves. Eventually the animal become tired of this treatment and revel. Upon a succesfull rebellion they are filled with hope for the future where they rule themselves. Little did they know that nothing would change.

"All animals are equal, but some animals are more equal than others"
